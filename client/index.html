Student Check-in

How to Use This App:

  1. Upload Roster: Click "Choose File" to select a CSV file (ID,FirstName,LastName) and then "Upload Roster" to load your student list.
  2. Scan QR Codes: Use the camera feed below to scan student QR codes. The app will automatically check them in.
  3. Manage Roster: Edit student names/IDs or delete students directly from the "Student Roster" list. You can also multi-select and delete.
  4. Persistence: Your roster and checked-in students are saved in your browser and will remain even if you close the page.
